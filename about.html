About Me!

I am a Fresh UX designer, a not so Fresh Musician and a professional Wanderer - looking for opportunites to design things that make people feel good.

I have made a huge Career shift from NHS administration to UX design with the help of the CareerFoundry UX Immersion course.

My years of experience working in the NHS have turned me into a natural problem solver and my passions outside of work keep my creative energy going.

As a UX Designer, I now combine the two: Problem Solving and Creativity.
